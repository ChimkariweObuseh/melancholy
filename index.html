Melancholy
Something Left To Feel — Omori

She plays the piano, fingers

Flowing as an ocean

Smoothly and slowly over

The arranged keys,

Melding together

A convergence of abstraction

And order.

She remembered times past.

She remembered

The swing she would play on.

She remembered the sun

Melting the ice cream

Off of her face, the warmth

Of those around her

Reminding her

That she was safe.

She remembered

When she was free.

She plays the piano, the notes

Falling as rain

On a tiled floor, constant

And persistent

And real.

She peaked out from under

Her umbrella.

She could feel the cold,

The ice, the pressing pushing ice

From every direction

But it was real.

So it was okay.

She remembered her friends

Cold like she was

In their large boots.

But they were together.

So it was okay.

She plays the piano, a smile

Playing at her lips, her closed eyes

Curving downwards as happiness

Enlightens her face.

Her visage

Shows only perfect joy

Except for the tears.

She used to play the piano.
